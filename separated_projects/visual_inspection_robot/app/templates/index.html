Visual Inspection Robot Car
\n
\n Welcome to the control center of the Autonomous Visual Inspection Car. This system utilizes a two-wheeled differential drive chassis integrated with ROS2 for messaging, OpenCV computer vision for line tracking and marker alignment, and a secure Flask control terminal.\n
\n\n
\n
\n
Line Following Route
\n

\n Follows physical black guides taped to the classroom floor autonomously, adjusting headings via real-time centroid offset calculations.\n

\n
\n
\n
Target Checkpoints
\n

\n Identifies horizontal crossbars, brings motors to a controlled stop, captures high-resolution camera frames of inspection targets, and logs photos dynamically.\n

\n
\n
\n
ROS2 Node Structure
\n

\n Communicates via decoupled nodes (`camera_node`, `vision_node`, `control_node`, `motor_node`, and `flask_bridge_node`) to distribute control processes.\n
